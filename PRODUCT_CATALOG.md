## 📈 Demand Levels

- **Very High**: Constant demand, best for bulk sales
  - Examples: Potato, Onion, Banana, Paneer, Chicken, Eggs, Milk
- **High**: Good demand, regular buyer interest
  - Examples: Tomato, Spinach, Mango, Strawberry, Honey
- **Medium**: Seasonal or specialty demand
  - Examples: Bell Pepper, Guava, Kale, Fish

## 💡 Quick Tips for Sellers

1. **Best Sellers**: Focus on Very High demand items for consistent income
2. **Seasonal Planning**: Grow Year Round items + seasonal specialties for variety
3. **Premium Products**: Microgreens (₹255-305/kg) and Fruits (₹155-245/kg) have higher margins
4. **Bulk Sales**: Animal products and grocery items work well in bulk

## 🔄 Product Management

To manage products, edit: `src/config/products.ts`

**Key Functions Available**:
- `getProductById(id)` - Get single product details
- `getProductsByCategory(category)` - Filter by category
- `getHighDemandProducts()` - Get high/very high demand items
- `getProductNames()` - Get all product names for dropdowns
- `searchProducts(query)` - Search by name or category
